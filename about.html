ABOUT ME

Hello World! My name is Andy Wang. I'm a senior at Purdue University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics. I also develop a variety of apps and games, which you can find under the 'Projects' tab.

I've always loved making things. From popsicles to Legos to foamboard to wood, the satisfaction I get from seeing an idea become reality is unparalleled. Nowadays, my medium of choice is programming. The number of impactful things a single developer can create is infinite, and that is why I chose to study computer science.

Some skills in my toolkit include: C#, Java, C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, React, MongoDB, and Unity.
